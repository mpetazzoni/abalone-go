How to Play Abalone

Objective

Push 6 of your opponent's marbles off the board to win.

The Board

Abalone is played on a hexagonal board with 61 spaces. Each player starts with 14 marbles arranged on opposite sides of the board. Black moves first.

Taking Turns

On your turn, select 1, 2, or 3 of your marbles and move them one space in any of the 6 directions.

Types of Moves

  • In-line: Move 1, 2, or 3 marbles along their shared axis. The front marble moves into the next space, and the others follow.
  • Broadside: Move 2 or 3 marbles sideways together. All destination spaces must be empty.

Pushing (Sumito)

When moving in-line, if your group outnumbers adjacent opponent marbles in the direction of movement, you push them. Legal pushes: 2 vs 1, 3 vs 1, or 3 vs 2.

Pushed marbles move one space in the push direction. If a pushed marble goes off the edge of the board, it's captured!

Controls

  1. Click your marbles to select them (1–3 marbles, must be in a line)
  2. Direction arrows appear around your selection
  3. Click an arrow to make your move

Click a selected marble again to deselect it.

Winning

The first player to push 6 of their opponent's marbles off the board wins the game!
